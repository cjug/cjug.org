Be a Sponsor

One goal of the CJUG is to allow developers to meet, discuss and learn from our regularly scheduled presentations. These meetings are a fantastic opportunity to connect or network with recruiters and the Chicago tech industry, if one is so inclined. Benefits of this symbiotic relationship between developers and recruiters includes career opportunities, different event hosting locations, raffles, freebies and discounts. Many years ago CJUG relied on membership fees to cover a minimal set of expenses. With anticipated growth from a events for the developer community, including the ability to invite guest speakers from outside of Chicago.

If you are a Java professional you should expect to see more benefits from future CJUG activities and the quality of our presentation events. There will be “rules of engagement” between our paying sponsors and you as a member, in general we don’t want members to be overwhelmed by sponsors, so we try to make attendance to this portion of the event separate and optional.

If you are interested in becoming a sponsor we are offering multiple levels of privileges (shared or exclusive) such as speaking or presenting briefly at a meeting, emailing the user list, hosting opportunities and other advertising or marketing opportunities through CJUG. Note that this means we are closing the “free” channels of advertising such as the now disabled chicago-java-advertise@yahoogroups.com, Jobs and Promotions on the LinkedIn group, etc. Please see below for pricing and contact us through the http://cjug.org web site or email cjug@cjug.org.

Based on feedback from our members, attendees, and sponsors, they have asked us to eliminate any sponsor attendee’s that are not paid sponsors. Thus we have a policy of not allowing non-paid sponsors to attend, they will be asked to leave.

3 important facts that may interest you…

  1. CJUG, founded in 1995, is one of the oldest ORACLE JAVA USER GROUPS in country
  2. CJUG is the 2nd Largest JAVA USER GROUP in country – with literally thousands of Illinois Java Professionals!
  3. If you only get one person from CJUG – sponsorship more than pays for itself!

Sponsorship Levels

Platinum Level ($20,000 or board approved equivalent of goods and services to CJUG.)

Branding presentations and track with sponsor information.
Company and job posting information on CJUG website along with sponsor directed email.
15 minute presentation before sponsored track.
All Events for track hosted at sponsor designated locations.
Sponsor brand awareness to members (full access to email list, attendee list).

Gold Level ($5,000 or board approved equivalent of goods and services to CJUG.)

Two “directed” presentations.
One Company and job posting information with 2 sentence message to be sent with event announcement.
One 15 minute approved presentation.
Event hosted at your location.
3 emails to our member base on behalf of your company.

Silver Level ($ 2,500 or board approved equivalent of goods and services to CJUG.)

One “directed” presentation.
Company information to be sent with event email announcement.
A 10 minute approved presentation.

Bronze Level ($ 750 or board approved equivalent of goods and services to CJUG.)

Sponsorship of one CJUG meeting (venue, location, time, and track chosen by CJUG).
Company sponsorship noted on website.
